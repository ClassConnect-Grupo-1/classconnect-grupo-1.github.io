ClassConnect User Guide

An overview of how our backoffice and mobile app operates

Overview

ClassConnect is a mobile app where teachers and students can join/create courses to improve their learning experience.

Backoffice

The backoffice system is designed to help administrators and internal staff manage platform operations easily.

  • User Management: View and update users. An admin can change any user's role (teacher or student) and can block or unblock a user.
  • Metrics: View metric diagrams about logins, notifications, or course approvals per user and per day. To do this, select a range of dates from the filter panel.

Mobile App

The mobile application is designed to support both students and teachers in their educational journey with seamless, on-the-go access to learning resources and class management tools.

  • Authentication: Users can log in via email and password, or federated identity providers such as Google. Secure PIN verification during registration is also supported. You will be asked to choose a role (student or teacher).
  • Profile Management: View and edit your profile, see your own data or public information from other users.
  • Course Access: Access all your courses. Mark courses as favorites for quick navigation.
  • Enrollment: Browse a catalog of available courses with filters and descriptions, and enroll directly through the app.
  • Content & Resources: Access course materials, participate in practical assignments (TPs), and view modules structured by educators.
  • Assignments & Exams: Complete tasks and take exams. Teachers can view lists of submissions, grade, and provide feedback.
  • Feedback System: Both students and teachers can give and receive structured feedback.
  • Communication: Push/email notifications keep users informed of important updates.
For the best experience, ensure the app is updated to the latest version via Google Play.
